Who Is The Consultation For?

Chicago's free medical check-up and weight loss consultation is for Chicago residents who are looking to lose weight so they can live a long, vibrant and healthy life, while looking good and not breaking the bank.

How Does It Work?

In this exam a certified nurse or physician will do various tests to check your overall health level and find which easy-to-do physician-supervised weight loss program will work best for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is it free?

We offer free consultations to make our services accessible to as many people as possible. Our goal is to help Chicago residents achieve a healthier lifestyle without worrying about the cost. This initial consultation allows you to understand our approach and see if our programs are right for you, with no financial obligation.

Do I need this if I've had my yearly medical check? What is the difference?

Even if you've had your yearly medical check, our consultation is specifically focused on weight loss and overall health improvement. It includes tests and evaluations tailored to creating a personalized weight loss program. This goes beyond a standard medical check-up, which might not focus on weight management.

Can I bring another person to the appointment?

Yes, you can bring a friend or family member for support. Having someone with you can help you feel more comfortable and they might also benefit from learning about our services.

How long does it take?

The initial consultation typically takes about 30 to 45 minutes. During this time, you'll meet with a certified nurse or physician who will conduct various health assessments and discuss your weight loss goals.

What if I have to cancel or if I'm not sure I'll be able to make it?

If you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment, please let us know as soon as possible. We understand that things come up and we will do our best to accommodate your schedule.

Do I need to bring anything with me?

You don't need to bring much, but if you have any recent medical records or a list of medications you're currently taking, that would be helpful. Otherwise, just bring yourself and a positive attitude!

What if I don't have insurance?

No insurance is required for the free consultation. Our programs are designed to be affordable and accessible, regardless of your insurance status.

Do the weight loss programs you offer work?

Yes, our weight loss programs are physician-supervised and have helped many individuals achieve their health goals. They are tailored to each person's needs, ensuring that you get a plan that works specifically for you.

What's the catch?

There is no catch. We believe in transparency and genuinely want to help you achieve your weight loss and health goals. The free consultation is our way of introducing you to our services and showing you how we can support you.

Will I have to exhaust myself in the gym to make it work?

Not at all. Our programs are designed to be practical and sustainable. While physical activity is a component, we focus on a balanced approach that includes diet, lifestyle changes, and support to ensure you can maintain your progress without feeling overwhelmed.

Where are you located?

We are located in Chicago. You can find us at 1931 W 95th Streeet Chicago IL, 60643. Feel free to contact us for more detailed directions or to schedule your consultation.
